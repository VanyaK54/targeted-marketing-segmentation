Targeted Marketing Using Customer Segmentation – Project Summary

📌 Objective:
Use clustering to segment retail customers for personalized campaigns (email, ads).

📊 Methodology:
- Cleaned raw customer and transaction data
- Engineered features: average spend, total transactions, encoded demographics
- Performed KMeans clustering with PCA (n=4)
- Generated segment-wise campaign strategies

🎯 Insights:
- Segment 0: High spenders with low response rates – premium offerings
- Segment 1: Bargain hunters – flash deals & coupons
- Segment 2: New/inactive users – welcome campaigns
- Segment 3: Loyalty members – retention campaigns

📈 Power BI Dashboard:
Visualizes KPIs, responses, and trends by segment
